The Lakeshore Community Chorus, an adult non-profit organization located in the Saugatuck/Douglas region of Western Michigan, is dedicated to the performance of varied choral music for the benefit, education and enjoyment of its members and surrounding lakeshore communities.

Our chorus is thrilled to be able to perform at various venues throughout our area. We offer a concert at the end of our two sessions every year and sing at a variety of local functions as well. If your group is looking for musical entertainment, please use our Contact Us page to request more information.
